Our Health Care Staffs

REGISTERED NURSES (RN)

They are highly qualified nurses competent in comprehensive assessments and developing individualized care plans. They deliver nursing care to patients, offer health education programs, and respond to consultative needs within the scope of their license.

REGISTERED PRACTICAL NURSES (RPN)

They provide direct care within their full scope of practice and participate in health care services, continuous monitoring, and evaluation of patients and their families. As an interdisciplinary team member, the RPN considers patients' social determinants of health and person-centred care approach.

PERSONAL SUPPORT WORKERS (PSW)

Care assistants support patients with personal hygiene and foster productive communication. They provide health care assistance and offer dedicated and compassionate care, allowing patients to live comfortably with dignity and self-esteem.

SERVICES WE PROVIDE

We offer a wide range of services, from individualized home-based care to advanced comprehensive nursing care. A variety of care options are available for the elderly, ranging from 24-hour nursing services to visiting nurses who visit older patients for a few hours each day for the following, although not limited to :

  • Assistance in personal hygiene and activities of daily living
  • Medication management
  • Physical activity and mobility at home or around healthcare facilities.
  • Feeding assistance
  • Safe environment and fall prevention
  • communication and transfer of accountability of care
  • Companionship and socialization
  • Mental health care

Our Health Care Staffs

REGISTERED NURSES (RN)

Our team comprises highly qualified nurses proficient in conducting comprehensive assessments and crafting individualized care plans. They administer nursing care to patients, facilitate health education programs, and address consultative needs within the scope of their licensure.

REGISTERED PRACTICAL NURSES (RPN)

Our professionals offer direct care within their full scope of practice, actively engaging in healthcare services, continuous monitoring, and the evaluation of both patients and their families. As integral members of an interdisciplinary team, our RPNs take into account the social determinants of health and embrace a person-centered care approach.

PERSONAL SUPPORT WORKERS (PSW)

Our care assistants play a crucial role in supporting patients with personal hygiene and cultivating effective communication. They deliver healthcare assistance with a dedicated and compassionate approach, enabling patients to live comfortably while preserving their dignity and self-esteem.

SERVICES WE PROVIDE

We provide an extensive array of services, spanning individualized home-based care to advanced comprehensive nursing services. Our care options for the elderly are diverse, including 24-hour nursing services and visits from nurses who cater to older patients for a few hours each day. These services encompass, though are not limited to:

  • Support with personal hygiene and activities of daily living
  • Management of medications
  • Encouragement of physical activity and mobility at home or within healthcare facilities
  • Assistance with feeding
  • Ensuring a safe environment and implementing fall prevention measures
  • Facilitating communication and transfer of accountability of care
  • Providing companionship and opportunities for socialization
  • Addressing mental health care needs
